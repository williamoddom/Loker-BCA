About Us

Bank Central Asia (BCA) is one of the largest private banks in Indonesia. Established on February 21, 1957, BCA has grown into a financial institution offering a wide range of banking services, including retail banking, corporate banking, and investment services. BCA was initially founded by Salim Group, but after the 1998 financial crisis, its ownership changed. Today, the majority of BCA's shares are owned by PT Dwimuria Investama Andalan, controlled by the Hartono family (owners of Djarum Group).

Our Services

Bank Central Asia (BCA) offers a wide range of banking services tailored for individuals, businesses, and corporations. For personal banking, BCA provides various savings accounts, such as Tahapan BCA, Tahapan Xpresi, and BCA Dollar, as well as deposit options like time deposits and foreign currency deposits. Customers can also access debit and credit cards, loans (such as home and vehicle loans), and investment products including mutual funds, bonds, and insurance. To enhance convenience, BCA offers digital banking solutions like BCA Mobile, myBCA, KlikBCA (internet banking), and QRIS payments, ensuring seamless transactions.

For businesses and corporations, BCA provides current accounts, payroll services, business internet banking, and auto debit facilities. Companies can also benefit from business loans, working capital financing, and investment credit. Additionally, BCA supports foreign exchange transactions, trade finance, and international remittances for global business operations. With advanced security measures like KeyBCA and biometric authentication, alongside customer support through Halo BCA 1500888 and live chat, BCA continues to innovate and deliver secure, efficient, and customer-friendly banking services

Tentang Rekrutmen Bank Central Asia (BCA)

Bank Central Asia (BCA) membuka kesempatan bagi para lulusan SMA/SMK serta Fresh Graduate untuk bergabung dengan salah satu bank swasta terbesar di Indonesia. Tahun 2025 ini, BCA mencari individu yang dinamis dan berorientasi pada pelayanan untuk mengisi berbagai posisi di seluruh jaringan kantor BCA. Ini adalah peluang karir yang menjanjikan di industri perbankan yang terus berkembang.


Posisi yang Dibutuhkan

BCA membutuhkan tenaga kerja untuk berbagai posisi layanan dan operasional, termasuk:

  • Teller
  • Customer Service
  • Back Office Staff
  • Posisi pendukung operasional perbankan lainnya

Kandidat yang berhasil akan bertanggung jawab dalam memberikan pelayanan prima kepada nasabah dan mendukung kelancaran operasional bank di berbagai cabang di seluruh Indonesia.


Kualifikasi yang Dibutuhkan

Pelamar diharapkan memenuhi kualifikasi berikut:

  • Pendidikan: Minimal lulusan SMA/SMK. Fresh Graduate dipersilakan melamar.
  • Kemampuan:
    • Kemampuan komunikasi yang baik.
    • Kemampuan melayani pelanggan dengan ramah dan profesional.
    • Kemampuan bekerja dalam tim.
    • Ketelitian dan kemampuan memecahkan masalah.
  • Pengalaman Kerja: Tidak diperlukan pengalaman kerja sebelumnya. Fresh Graduate sangat dianjurkan untuk melamar.

Gaji dan Tunjangan

Gaji Pokok akan disesuaikan dengan posisi dan tingkat pengalaman. BCA menawarkan paket kompensasi yang kompetitif di industri perbankan.

Selain gaji pokok, BCA juga menyediakan berbagai tunjangan dan manfaat sebagai bagian dari kompensasi, termasuk:
